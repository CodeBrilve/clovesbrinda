QA - quality Assurance Welding Inspector

This was a project I started back in November 2011 At ArcelorMittal Projects. I was the responsible for performed testing of products at specific stages to ensure meeting of quality measurements including dimensions performance and correct chemical makeup.

Inspecting welds for quality standards also performed statistical analyses using computer models to test conformance to specifications.

Reading and interpreting engineering documents, Worked as a part of the management team to achieve increased productivity while reducing error rates and rejected inferior products and inferior welds. Recommended discipline for welders who submitted consistently inferior products including firing or remedial training.

Training welders in quality welding and performed random inspections of products to ensure quality standards were met. Reducing product and weld error rates by 30 percent through implementation of a quality control system furthermore Interpreting testing data recommending action to the plant manager.

I worked on this project for 5 years.
